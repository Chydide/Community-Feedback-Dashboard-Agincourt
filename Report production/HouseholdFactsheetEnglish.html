Kildare A Village Factsheet 2018

Population

In 2018, there were 4831 people living in 993 households, with 2329 men and 2502 women.

Education

In 2018, there were 4831 people living in 993 households, with 2329 men and 2502 women.

The elderly

In 2018 there were 414 people over the age of 60. Of this there were 153 men and 261 women.

Births

In 2018, there were a total of 42 babies born, and 20 were boys, and 22 were girls.

Deaths

In 2018, a total of 20 people died, 8 were male and 12 women.

Migration

In 2018, 53 people moved into the village and 93 moved out.

This fact sheet provides basic information on the population - numbers, births, deaths and migration of people in your village. More detailed information can be obtained upon request from the MRC/Wits-Agincourt Unit.
